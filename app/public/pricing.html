Pricing FAQ

Can I switch plans anytime?

Yes. Upgrade or downgrade anytime. Pro-rated refunds for annual plans.

What happens if I exceed Free tier limits?

You'll see a soft limit warning at 80 calls/day. At 100, rate limiting kicks in. Upgrade to Premium for unlimited access.

Do you offer academic/nonprofit discounts?

Yes. Contact us at jay@kinuchat.com with proof of status for 50% off Premium or custom Enterprise pricing.

Is there a free trial?

The Free tier is permanent and fully functional. No credit card required. Upgrade when you need more.
