**"Is this just another wrapper?"**
No. Wrappers give you a chat box. Tiker gives you task management, agent coordination, and persistent memory. Your agents work as a team.

**"What if I want different AI models?"**
Use whatever you want. Anthropic, OpenAI, Google â€” set your keys, pick your defaults.
